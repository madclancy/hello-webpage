Meet Peaches

Peaches is a 5 year old cat located in Minneapolis, MN. She is so friendly, and always wants to be wherever you are. There's no doubt that she would be a lovely partner for a special someone.

Stay tuned to learn about her and see if your pet might just be the right fit!

Day in the Life

Peaches' day starts out by her climbing on top of her owners and meowing for attention and feeding. Once fed, she spends her day hanging out with her sister. They will play with their many toys, and scratch the couch. After a long day of playing, she cozies up for a midday nap in her favorite shoebox and awaits dinnertime. Post dinner, she makes sure she is always on someone's lap, and receiving constant pets. She'll end the day cozied up in bed, taking up an astonishing amount of space for such a small animal.

Pictured above: Peaches paws deep in a romance novel, as she tends to be most evenings.

What She's Looking For

What Peaches is searching for is what we all want in life, a companion, friend, and fellow cat to share her little shoebox with. She is looking for loyalty, kindness, and someone who can provide. No cats over the age of ten please.
